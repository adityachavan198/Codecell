As the very same suggests, in hackathon we try to use tech to hack into real world problems to solve them better. It is a marathon of building, creating, producing and delivering a product in a short amount of time.


Evaluation criteria will include novelty of the idea, complexity, clarity and details in the prescribed format, feasibility, practicability, sustainability, scale of impact, user experience and potential for future work progression.


Each team should consist of two to four members including a team leader. The team members can be of any institute or branch.


Bring your student ID , laptop, charger, and anything you would need for a sleepover.

Any kind of weapons, drugs , alcohol are strictly not permitted.
