What is Codecell?

The Platform Where All Of Us Share The Love Of Coding. We Have Interesting Events Such As Reach & Teach Programs, Project Drives, Hackathons And Weekly Challenges Lined Up For You! Interact With Other Coders To Grow Your Knowledge Base And Get Your Hands On The Demanding Technologies In Industries.

Who are we?

We Helps You Understand The Coding World Outside Our College With This Platform Of Codecell, With The Help Of Our Platform, We Together Aim that The Members Of This Community Shall Grow To Become Better Coders! Introduce Yourself To The World Of Programming And Grow As You Code.

Our Events
